# Code of Conduct

**Purpose**  
Set expectations for community behavior and contribution.

## Our standards
- Be respectful and constructive.
- No harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language.
- Provide actionable feedback.

## Reporting
- Report violations to project maintainers privately.
- Maintain confidentiality during investigations.

## Enforcement
- Violations may result in warnings, temporary bans, or removal from the project.
