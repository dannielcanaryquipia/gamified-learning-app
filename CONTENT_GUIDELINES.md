# Content Guidelines

**Purpose**  
Standards and templates for lesson authors and content reviewers.

## Tone and length
- Tone: friendly, concise, and actionable.
- Lesson length: 300–800 words for micro-lessons.
- Use short paragraphs and clear headings.

## Frontmatter requirements
- `title`, `topicId`, `lessonId`, `xp`, `estimatedMinutes`, `locked`, `thumbnail`, `tags`

## Quiz rules
- Provide 2–5 questions per lesson.
- Use multiple-choice for quick checks; include one explanation per answer.

## Media and assets
- Host images in `assets/content/` and reference with relative paths.
- Optimize images for mobile.

## Accessibility
- Provide alt text for images.
- Avoid color-only cues.

## Review workflow
- Draft → Peer review → QA → Publish
- Use `content/drafts/` for AI-generated drafts and mark with `draft: true` in frontmatter.
